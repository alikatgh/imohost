imo Professional Host Program

The imo host programme enables you to earn cash bonuses by hosting engaging and entertaining audio events in Channels. The more active audience, the more gifters, the more people in the discussion, the higher your cash bonus payout.

Other benefits:

  • Channels hosted by professional hosts get additional traffic boost.
  • Direct interaction and support from imo team officials.
  • Mentorship and training opportunity from other experienced imo hosts in the community.

Apply to be a Professional Host:

  1. Go to a voice room, and tap the host recruitment banner on the bottom right hand corner of the screen.
  2. Fill out the application form with your details and wait for an interview notification.

Organise an event:

  1. Organise an event using the ‘My event’ feature on imo.
  2. Engaging event will be noticed and you will be contacted by an official staff member.
  • Respond to the questionnaire honestly and give some thought to it.
  • We are looking for people who are passionate about imo, enjoy organizing people together, and are knowledgeable about a unique topic.
  • If you are organising an event, an event with many people, many speakers, and many gifters will give you the potential to be selected.
  1. Go to your profile page, scroll down, and tap the “my event” feature.
  2. You need to be a Channel owner or a Channel super admin in order to see this.
  3. On the event page, enter your event details and tap submit.
  4. How to fill up this form … (detailed explanations can be added here).
  • Channel Awards Ceremony: Use this option if you want to have fun or celebrate an occasion with your friends and family online.
  • Game: Use this option if the main theme of your programme is to play games with each other.
  • Talkshow: Use this option if you will be discussing a fixed topic (not random conversation) with your friends or your followers.
  • Friend-making: Use this option if you want to open a room to connect people together. This is good for networking or general relationship building.
  • Competition: Use this option when you want to organise a competitive atmosphere (Such as PK battles) for participants in your room. The winners may receive gifts, while the losers may have to send gifts.
  • Talk Show: Use this option when you want to discuss a certain topic with a group of other speakers.
  • Talent Show: Use this option to showcase yours or other people’s talents. It can be singing, or story telling, or reciting a poetry, or special ASMR, or play your music instrument.
  • Radio: Use this option if you would like you to share your thoughts, ideas, or opinion to an audience.
  • Couple Wedding: Use this option if you want to have fun match making or celebrating the relationship between two (or many) people.

  • You can also share your event in your follower group to inform them that an event is going to be held and that they are invited.
  • Share the event on your imo stories so that other people can see your upcoming event too.
  • Work with your friends from other groups to cross-promote each of your events together.
  • During your event, if it is engaging (many people attending, many people commenting, and many people talking), and it happens to receive gifts from multiple different people, the algorithm will also allocate additional promotion to you automatically.
  • Bonus: If you are a Professional Host, your event will automatically be boosted more than 10x your usual volume.
